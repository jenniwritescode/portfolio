Dear recruiters, head hunters and HR people!

Recruiting is hard, finding good candidates is even harder. I am totally aware, since I've been involved in the hiring & interviewing process myself. While I am not currently looking for a new position, maybe you have the absolutely amazing position I would be interested in... To make the whole process as low effort as possible and not a waste of time for both of us, here are a few things to consider when contacting me with a job offer: I live in Chicago and will not be relocating. I am not interested in any positions that are not fully remote. I am not interested in contract or freelance work. I won't reply to any mass messages. I don't work for early stage start-up companies. Please provide a full job description. Please provide a salary range for the position, including bonus where applicable. Please understand that I am not willing to change jobs for less than what I am earning now. After you have considered above points, I would be happy to discuss further - in case the position is absolutely appealing. Thank you for your consideration, in order to not waste your and my time. Kind Regards, Jenni Hartman
