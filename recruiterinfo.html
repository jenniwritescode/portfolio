A Note for Recruiters, Head Hunters and HR People

While I am not currently looking for a new position, maybe you have an absolutely amazing position I would be interested in...

To make the whole process as low effort as possible and not a waste of time for both of us, here are a few things to consider when contacting me regarding a position:

  • I am not interested in any positions that are not fully remote. This is non-negotiable.
  • I am not interested in contract or freelance work.
  • I live in Chicago and will not be relocating.
  • I don't reply to any mass messages.
  • I don't work for early stage start-up companies.
  • I can't consider a role without a full job description.
  • I can't consider a role without knowing the salary range, including bonus where applicable.
  • Please understand that I am not willing to change jobs for less than what I am earning now.

After reviewing the above points, if you believe I would be interested in what you have to offer, please reach out and I would be happy to discuss further.

Thank you for your consideration!

Kind Regards,
Jenni
